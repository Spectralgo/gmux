# M4 - Persistence, Review & Advanced Integrations

M4 layers durable recovery, persistent terminal options, review tooling, and platform integrations onto the core Gmux cockpit.

## Included epics

- [EPIC-012 - Session persistence and semantic resume](../epics/EPIC-012-session-persistence-and-semantic-resume.md)
- [EPIC-013 - Persistent terminal modes and review UX](../epics/EPIC-013-persistent-terminal-modes-and-review-ux.md)
- [EPIC-014 - MCP and multi-profile foundation](../epics/EPIC-014-mcp-and-multi-profile-foundation.md)

## Exit criteria

- Users can recover layouts and named sessions predictably.
- Gmux exposes opinionated review and diff surfaces tied to Gastown work contexts.
- Agent-facing integrations and multi-profile isolation have stable platform boundaries.

## Not in scope

- Reworking the earlier cockpit model or replacing Beads as the system of record.
- Broad team analytics beyond the integrations explicitly listed in this milestone.
