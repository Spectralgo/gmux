# US-008 - Ready Work And Bead Context

## Persona

Operator or contributor deciding what to pick up next.

## Narrative

As a user, I want ready work and bead detail in Gmux so I can understand dependencies and next actions without leaving the app.

## Why it matters

Beads readiness and bead detail are core domain signals; hiding them behind an external CLI reduces the value of the cockpit.

## Acceptance criteria

- A ready-work surface shows what can move now using Beads semantics.
- Bead detail shows dependencies, current status, and audit-relevant context.
- The surfaces can be opened from convoy, workspace, and notification flows.
