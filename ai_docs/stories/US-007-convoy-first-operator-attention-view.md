# US-007 - Convoy-First Operator Attention View

## Persona

Town operator monitoring active work across rigs.

## Narrative

As an operator, I want Gmux to open into a convoy-first attention view so I can see stranded or progressing work immediately.

## Why it matters

The research anchors convoys as the primary attention unit for multi-rig work; the product should reflect that model directly.

## Acceptance criteria

- Active convoys are visible as a first-class dashboard surface.
- Selected convoy detail includes tracked issues, status, and progress.
- Stranded or attention-worthy convoys are surfaced explicitly.
