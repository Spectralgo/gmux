# US-013 - Keep Sessions Alive And Review Changes In Product

## Persona

Operator or reviewer juggling long-running sessions and merge decisions.

## Narrative

As a user, I want persistent session options and in-product review surfaces so I can keep work alive across restarts and inspect changes without context switching.

## Why it matters

Persistent terminals and integrated review remove two of the highest-friction workflows for multi-agent operations.

## Acceptance criteria

- Supported persistent terminal backends are available with explicit tradeoffs.
- Review surfaces show enough diff or changed-file context to guide decisions.
- Review flows remain tied to the active Gastown identity and workspace context.
